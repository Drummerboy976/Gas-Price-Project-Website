The rising prices of gas in California

By Fausto and Ronnie

Context

Gas prices have been at an all-time high, and that even more true for the state of California. California ranks number one in the nation for highest gas prices. There are many factors that attribute to this, including rising gas prices, the COVID epidemic, and the invasion of Ukraine by Russia - just to name a few. There's also the issue of inflation, raising the prices of everything we buy. Add the additional costs of owning a vehicle as well, we can afford to cut back at the gas pump and save some money.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about the current rise of gas prices here in the state of California as well as provide information about the different alternative methods of transportaion. Alternate modes of transportation can help alleviate the burden on gasoline and help individuals save money in the long run. California is known to be one of the most congested states in the country and using alternate forms of transportation can not only help individuals with savings, but also help reduce the amount of traffic, especially in big cities like Los Angeles and San Francisco.
